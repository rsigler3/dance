Our Legacy

Cultivating Artistry

Welcome to the Chautauqua Regional Youth Ballet. As the region's elite hub for classical ballet, we dedicate ourselves to the pursuit of technical precision and profound artistry.

Our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to foster discipline, grace, and an enduring passion for dance in every student, from our Pre-Ballet classes to our Advanced conservatory levels.

Curriculum & Programming

Comprehensive training for every stage of your dance journey.

Pre-Ballet

An introduction to movement, spatial awareness, and the foundational joy of dance for young aspiring artists.

Classical Instruction

Rigorous, tiered syllabus rooted in the timeless techniques of classical ballet for dedicated students.

Summer Intensives

Immersive summer programming designed to rapidly elevate technique and performance quality.
